This is not a real ecommerce website!

This site was built as a study project with the Django Framework. You cannot order real guitars here nor is its intention that the configuration options and images are accurate.

It is meant to be a proof of concept of what a guitar configurator could look like.

Feel free to register and play around with the configurator or start configuring as a guest. If you register you don't have to give away sensitive informations and your password will be hashed.

This site uses cookies for the sole purpose of making it possible the site works as a unregistered user. No personal information will be stored.
